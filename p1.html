About the artist

Nicole Yuen

Nicole has a keen desire to humanize technology by combining anthropomorphic items with images and videos of human beings. With technological components she wants her work to reflect the relationship we have with technology and how it can beneficially affect us. Yuen’s main concentrations are using augmented reality, virtual reality and Arduino technology to explore how they can improve our everyday lives.

Yuen interest transitioned from pictorial to digital during her studies at San Jose State University. As a Digital Media Arts student, she was able to expand her skills through interdisciplinary and discovered her passion for technology. She works with software such as 3D modeling with Autodesk Maya, Blender, Unity and Cinema 4D. Languages she learned for creative coding are with Arduino, Html, and Javascript. What she enjoys about digital art is that there are also hardware such HTC Vives, 3D printers, Kinects, and electronic circuitry to allow her to experiment with.

Through Yuen’s practice, she tries to humanize technology and examine how humans interact with them. With digital art becoming the new media she explores the endless possibilities of how technology can be utilized as a tool to improve our lives.
